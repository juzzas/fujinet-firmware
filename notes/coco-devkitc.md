# Wiring for CoCo FujiNet that uses ESP32-DEVKITC

```
CASSETTE 1 - CASSETTE MOTOR GND     (WHITE/BRN)
CASSETTE 2 - CASSETTE GND           (ORANGE)
CASSETTE 3 - CASSETTE MOTOR CONTROL (BROWN)
CASSETTE 4 - CASSETTE DATA IN       (WHITE/BLUE)
CASSETTE 5 - CASSETTE DATA OUT      (BLUE


SERIAL 1 --- SERIAL CARRIER DETECT  (WHITE/ORANGE)
SERIAL 2 --- COCO RX                (WHITE/GREEN)
SERIAL 3 --- GND                    (GREEN)
SERIAL 4 --- COCO TX                (ORANGE)


    5v ----- LM386 Vcc
   3v3 ----- CASSETTE CABLE MOTOR GND (WHITE/BRN)
   GND ----- SERIAL CABLE GND (GREEN) & LM386 GND
GPIO21 ----- SERIAL CABLE RX (WHITE/GREEN)
GPIO25 ----- LM386 INPUT (DAC1)
GPIO33 ----- SERIAL CABLE TX (ORANGE)
GPIO34 ----- CASSETTE CABLE MOTOR (BROWN)

LM386 GND -- CASSETTE GND (ORANGE
LM386 OUT -- CASSETTE DATA IN (WHITE/BLUE)

UNRESOLVED: SERIAL CARRIER DETECT
```
